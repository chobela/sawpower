Background

Sawpower Company was incorporated in 2006 and has been servicing the Hotel Industry for more than 13 years by providing and servicing quality and durable Vulcan catering equipment. The Company has also been servicing the hospitality industry in general and in additional to this has also been supplying saw blades and machinery to the timber industry.

With years of experience, we provide reliable and durable products that have helped clients achieve their goals with great customer service and satisfaction. We are available for taking on new work.

  • Our products range from:

  • - Preparation counters, Stainless steel tables, Sinks
  • - Vegetable preparation machines
  • - Range cookers and Fry-top griddles – gas and electric
  • - Deep Fat Fryers, Pressure fryers
  • - Boiling pans, Tilt pans
  • - Salamanders and Conveyor oven toasters
  • - Rational™ Combination steam ovens and Self-Cooking Centre
  • - Bain Marie hot cupboards, Food warming and display equipment

  • Why We are Different

  • Most suppliers on the market will sell the equipment you ask for, but this creates 2 problems for you.
    1. You will need to look for someone to do the installation
    2. In case of a malfunction of the equipment you will need to look for someone to fix the problem.

The problem is compounded by the fact that most of the equipment is of unknown brands and In most of the cases the equipment does not come up with back up spares and or spares may not even be readily available.

Being a dealer for Vulcan Catering Equipment of South Africa we offer a very renowned brand of equipment that gives our clients value for money. In addition to this we have a crop of technicians that are trained by Vulcan and are able to resolve any problem that any piece of equipment may have. It is this passion we have for maintaining our equipment that separates us from our competitors.

Our after sales service is unmatched because unlike our competitors, we believe that our relationship with our customer does not end once our machines are delivered, we believe that delivery of the machine is only just the beginning. We make sure that the machine we deliver does precisely what it was made for, to work efficiently. Our technical team is not only highly qualified but experienced to handle most if not all technical problems that may arise. No job is too small or too large for us.
